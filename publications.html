Publications

Research contributions in video coding, compression, and distributed systems. Click on a publication to view details.

IEEE Signal Processing Letters, 2025.

This paper reformulates H.265/HEVC video enhancement as a signal recovery task from incomplete transform-domain measurements and integrates a modified ISTA framework into the decoding process. By replacing matrix operations with codec-aware encoding/decoding operators and a neural thresholding stage, the method achieves up to 1 dB PSNR gain over state-of-the-art enhancement techniques.

IEEE DSPA, 2025.

We propose a DVC method that selects the best side information using feedback from the error-correction decoder. By choosing the SI requiring the fewest syndrome bits for successful decoding, the system adapts to motion complexity and improves compression efficiency.

View on IEEE Xplore
IEEE RusAutoCon, 2024.

Proposed a Q-learning algorithm for real-time multipath routing in data centers, improving latency and throughput over ECMP/WCMP with reduced complexity compared to deep reinforcement learning.

View on IEEE Xplore
International Symposium on Problems of Redundancy in Information and Control Systems, 2025.

Proposes decoder-side methods that jointly exploit temporal (inter-frame) and spatial (inter-view) correlations to improve reconstruction quality in distributed multi camera video systems.
